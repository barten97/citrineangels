BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP

• Access to potential investments throughout the mid-Atlantic
• Prescreening of potential investments by experienced investors
• Opportunities to discuss potential investments with others in a collaborative setting
• Flexible investment amount
• A supportive environment for members without prior angel investing experience
• Anonymity and confidentiality, if you so choose


Membership Options Coming Soon

Access to potential investments throughout the mid-Atlantic

Our team sources potential investments from not only the DC metro area but also from other areas in the region, including Richmond, Virginia Beach/Norfolk, Baltimore, Wilmington and Philadelphia. Most individual investors do not have the time or inclination to do this themselves. We believe it’s beneficial and important to meet a founder in person so we limit our geographical reach accordingly.

Prescreening of potential investments by experienced investors.

One or more of the group’s executive committee members will preview pitches and narrow the field to those who are most investment-worthy to present to the group at one of our monthly meetings

Collaborative Setting

We believe group discussions among potential investors with a variety of backgrounds improves the decision-making process for both fledgling and experienced investors

Flexible Investment Amount

As a group, we have a greater ability to meet a founder’s minimum investment threshold, enabling members to make smaller investments than may be feasible otherwise

Supportive Enviroment

Our Angel Investing 101 course provides a learning experience for new members, and we will have educational sessions covering specific topics in more depth. We can also connect you to additional resources as you wish

Anonymity & Confidentiality

We will not publicize your involvement. If you would like to disclose your affiliation with the group, that is your choice

Who Qualifies To Join

Membership in Citrine Angels is intended for those individuals who self-identify as female

The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) requires that an angel investor self-certify that they qualify as an Accredited Investor under Rule 501 of Regulation D ("Reg D") of the Securities Act of 1933. An individual is required to have an individual net worth (including their spouse, if applicable) of more than $1 million, excluding their primary residence OR individual income of more than $200,000 (or joint income with their spouse of more than $300,000) in each of the two most recent years and a reasonable expectation of reaching the same income level in the current year. If you would like more detail on these regulations, please refer to www.sec.gov
